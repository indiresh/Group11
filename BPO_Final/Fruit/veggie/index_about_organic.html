Why Buy Organic?

There are many heath benefits buying organic for both the consumer and the planet. First off, organic foods reduce the amount of toxic chemicals ingested. They totallly avoid GMOs (genetically modified organisms) in which can cause long-term health problems. Organic grown foods also increases the amount of beneficial vitamins, minerals, essential fatty acids, and antioxidants consumed. There have also been studies linking eating organic foods to lowering the chances of getting cancer, coronary heart disease, allergies and hyperactivity in children.
